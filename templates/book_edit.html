Reading HTML

This block controls the summary on the book page and the structured sections on the read page.

Recommended structure

  • `<h1>` for the title.
  • One summary paragraph right after the title.
  • `<h2>` sections like `Plot & Themes` or `Literary Significance` for preview blocks.
  • A `Contents` section with links to reading anchors.
  • A `Text` section with `<section id="...">`, `<h3>`, and paragraphs.

What to avoid

  • Do not duplicate catalog metadata like author, languages, genre, or publication year here.
  • Keep IDs in contents links and text sections aligned, for example `href="#part1"` and `section id="part1"`.
  • If you remove `Contents` or `Text`, the read page will show an empty state instead of chapter links or text.
